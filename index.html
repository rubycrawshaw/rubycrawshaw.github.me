About Me

My name you already know is Ruby Crawshaw I am 18 years old. I was born and raised in Taranaki in a small place called Omata. I have lived on a large lifestyle block since I was two years old and have loved every minute of it. My parent are still together and love each other, and I am the oldest of four siblings. I have two brothers one 14 and one 12 and a younger sister who is 10. Yes they are the most annoying people to live with in all the world, but I love them.

Education

Primary

I first started school at the age of five, I went to Omata Primary Schhol in New PLymouth Taranaki. I was a keen artist and had a creative mind right from the start and was always getting into trouble.

College

I started college when I was thirteen and went to Spotswood College the only Co-Ed highschool in New Plymouth. I tookm heaps of art based classes and found that I was way better at painting and drawing than I was at Maths and English.

Now

I am now a student studying at Massey University, I plan on studying towards getting a Bachelor in Design and Visual Communication.

I have also had to move down to Wellington the capital of New Zealand, to be closer to the Wellington Massey University campus. I am adjusting to the Wellington student life, that being yelling at drunk people on the streets or finding the cheapest can of tomatoes to buy.

I am finding the student life can be a little tricky at times but mostly I feel like I am on top of things, like paying my bills and buying my weekly groceries.
